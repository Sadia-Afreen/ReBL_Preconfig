Bug Report Title:
Widget: New Note creates infinite new note loop

Bug Report Issue:
### Expected
Creating a new note by clicking on the FAB in the notes list widget will open a new blank note. Clicking "back" in the appbar should either close the app or take the user to the notes list. 

### Observed
Clicking "back" in the appbar creates a new note - ad infinitum 

### Reproduced
> 1. Tap the "+" FAB from within the notes list widget. The new app will open to a new note screen.
> 2. Tap the "back" button in the AppBar. Another new note will load. 
> 3. Continue to tap "back" and observe infinite new note loop.
